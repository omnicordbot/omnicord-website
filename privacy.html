1. Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy for Omnicord, a Discord bot. This document explains how we handle your data when you use our bot in a Discord server.

2. What Data We Collect

Omnicord may collect and process the following data:

  • Discord User Data: Username, user ID, and avatar.
  • Message Data: Only when required for bot features (e.g., moderation logs, custom commands).
  • Server Data: Server ID, roles, channels (only for bot-related functionality).
  • Interaction Data: Commands you use and bot responses.

We DO NOT collect passwords, private messages, or sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate the bot and respond to commands.
  • Improve functionality and bot features.
  • Ensure moderation and enforce server rules (if applicable).
  • Prevent abuse and protect user security.

4. Data Storage & Security

Omnicord stores minimal user data. Any stored data is:

  • Kept for as long as necessary to provide services.
  • Stored securely with restricted access.
  • Deleted when no longer needed or upon request.

We do not share or sell any user data.

5. Third-Party Services

Omnicord may interact with third-party services such as:

  • Discord API (for bot commands and interactions).
  • Logging services (only for moderation features).

We do not share user data with advertisers or external businesses.

6. How to Remove Your Data

If you wish to remove your data, you can:

  • Kick or ban the bot from your server.
  • Contact us at support@omnicord.com to request data deletion.

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable time.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, contact us at support@omnicord.com.
