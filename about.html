About Omnicord

Omnicord is a Discord bot that brings the power of the Omnitrix to your server. Transform into powerful aliens, battle epic enemies, and prove your skills in the Omniverse!

Meet the Creator: Phyniex

Omnicord was created and is maintained by Phyniex, a passionate developer with a deep love for both the Ben 10 franchise and Discord bots. Phyniex strives to deliver a unique experience by combining action-packed adventures with the interactive power of Discord.

Phyniex believes in creating bots that are both fun and functional, and Omnicord is no exception. With constant updates and new features, Phyniex ensures the bot stays fresh and engaging for all users.

If you want to learn more or just chat, feel free to reach out through the Support Server.
