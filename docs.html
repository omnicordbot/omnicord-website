Getting Started

Welcome to Omnicord! To begin, simply invite the bot to your server using our invite link and grant the necessary permissions.

Example:

Invite Omnicord ➔ Authorize ➔ Setup your first command!

Commands

Omnicord offers a wide variety of commands, from moderation to music! Here's how to use one:

/ban @user [reason] - Bans a user from the server.

/play [song name] - Plays a song in your voice channel.

API

Developers can interact with Omnicord's public API.

Example request:

GET https://api.omnicord.dev/v1/server/info
Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY

FAQ

Q: How do I reset my settings?

A: Simply use the /reset command or visit the dashboard.

Q: Is Omnicord open source?

A: Currently, Omnicord is closed source to ensure quality control and security.
