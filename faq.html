Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Omnicord

Getting Started

What is Omnicord?

Omnicord is the ultimate Ben 10 Discord bot that allows you to transform into aliens, battle villains, and compete on leaderboards. It brings the Omniverse experience to your Discord server with unique gameplay mechanics and premium features.

How do I add Omnicord to my server?

You can invite Omnicord to your server by:

  1. Clicking the "Invite" button on our website
  2. Selecting your server from the dropdown
  3. Granting the necessary permissions (we recommend using the default settings)
  4. Authorizing the bot to join

After joining, use /help to get started!

What permissions does Omnicord need?

Omnicord requires these permissions to function properly:

  • Read Messages/View Channels: To see commands and interactions
  • Send Messages: To respond to commands
  • Embed Links: For rich visual responses
  • Attach Files: For certain game assets
  • Use Slash Commands: For the command interface

We never request unnecessary permissions like administrator rights.

Gameplay

How do I unlock new aliens?

You can unlock new aliens by:

  • Collecting alien fragments through gameplay
  • Completing quests and challenges
  • Participating in special events
  • Trading with other players
  • Purchasing from the in-game market

Each alien requires a specific number of fragments to unlock.

How do battles work?

Omnicord features several battle types:

  • 1v1 Duels: Challenge other players directly
  • Villain Battles: Fight against AI-controlled villains
  • Guild Wars: Team battles between guilds

Battles are turn-based with strategic elements. Each alien has unique abilities and strengths. You can bet in-game currency on 1v1 matches.

What happens if I lose a battle?

Losing a battle has these consequences:

  • You may lose any currency you bet
  • Your battle stats will be recorded
  • You'll gain some experience points
  • No items or aliens are permanently lost

You can always challenge the opponent again or practice against villains to improve.

Economy & Items

How do I earn in-game currency?

You can earn currency through:

  • Winning battles
  • Completing daily quests
  • Selling items in the marketplace
  • Participating in events
  • Winning auctions
  • Guild rewards

Currency is server-specific and cannot be transferred between servers.

What's the difference between the market and auction?

Marketplace:

  • Fixed prices set by sellers
  • Instant purchases
  • Better for common items

Auction House:

  • Time-limited bidding
  • Potential for better deals
  • Better for rare items
  • 24-hour auction duration
What happens if I get scammed in a trade?

Omnicord does not mediate player trades or provide refunds for scams. However:

  • You can report scammers to server moderators
  • We recommend using the official marketplace for safer transactions
  • Always double-check trade details before confirming

Never share your account details or Discord tokens with anyone.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I see Omnicord's commands?

If commands aren't appearing, try these solutions:

  1. Ensure Omnicord has the correct permissions (see "What permissions does Omnicord need?")
  2. Type / in chat to trigger Discord's command menu
  3. Wait 1-2 minutes after adding the bot (commands may take time to register)
  4. Check if the bot is online in your server member list
  5. Try kicking and re-inviting the bot
How do I reset my progress?

Account resets are permanent and cannot be undone. To reset:

  1. Use the /reset command
  2. Confirm your choice
  3. Wait 24 hours for the reset to process

This will delete all your aliens, items, and currency. Proceed with caution.

How can I contact support?

For assistance, please:

  • Join our Support Server and open a ticket
  • Email support@omnicord.com
  • Check our Status Page for ongoing issues

Include your Discord ID and server information for faster resolution.
