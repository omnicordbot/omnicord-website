Frequently Asked Questions

What is Omnicord?
Omnicord is an advanced Discord bot designed to manage, moderate, and enhance your server experience with powerful features and sleek designs.
Is Omnicord free to use?
Yes! Omnicord is free to use for all servers. We also offer premium features for users who wish to support development.
How can I add Omnicord to my server?
Simply visit our website and click the "Invite" button. You'll be guided through the process to add Omnicord to your Discord server in seconds.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team through our Discord server. Just head to the #support channel and open a ticket.
